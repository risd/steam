STEM to STEAM

The problem-solving, the fearlessness, and critical thinking and making skills that define an art and design education fosters the creativity that will keep our country innovating, and their development needs to start in the K-12 schools. Creativity is the new literacy, and we need a well-rounded education system that develops the skills our economy needs.
